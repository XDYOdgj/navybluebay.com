USD167.99
LEVOIT LV600S combines smart humidification and air purification for pets. Features hybrid mist, 3-in-1 filters, and app control. Eliminates odors and allergens. Ideal for home and large rooms.

  • Reviews

  The LEVOIT LV600S Smart Warm and Cool Mist Humidifier is designed to bring comfort and health to your home, whether you're in your bedroom or a large living room. With its innovative hybrid mist technology, it delivers efficient humidity at 550ml/h, offering a more powerful and even distribution of moisture than traditional cool mist systems. This unit is ideal for people suffering from dry air or respiratory discomfort, as it maintains optimal humidity levels and works seamlessly with the Vesync App for customized settings and easy monitoring. The built-in sleep mode ensures quiet operation, perfect for light sleepers, while simultaneously turning off all lights for a peaceful, uninterrupted night’s rest.
  As an air purifier for pets, the LEVOIT LV600S helps create a healthier environment for your family. It features a high-efficiency activated carbon filter that neutralizes pet odors, litter box smells, and cooking smoke, with a deodorization rate of over 92%. Additionally, its 3-in-1 filtration system includes a non-woven fabric pre-filter, a HEPA filter, and an activated carbon filter, which together capture pet dander, dust, and airborne irritants. This reduces the need for frequent cleaning and helps protect your respiratory system, making it perfect for pet parents looking for a cleaner, more breathable home atmosphere.
